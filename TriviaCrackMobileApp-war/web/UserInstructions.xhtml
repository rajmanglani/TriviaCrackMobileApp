Athletic Training Certification Trivia

About the Game

Welcome to the Athletic Training Certifiation Trivia (ATCT) game. This game is not only entertaining but also educational and challenging. It will prepare you for your Certfication Exam by modeling the questions on the actual exam.

Rules

ATCT contains model questions from the Athectic Training Certification Exam in the form of multiple choice, fill in the blank and True/False questions. It contains the 5 common categories in the certification exam and lets you choose a category to begin the game.

Once you click on a category you are to answer each question within 20 seconds or else the question will be marked incorrect. Each category has 5 levels. You will be awarded one point for answering a level one question correctly, 2 points for a level 2 question and so on. If you get 5 correct answers then you will move a level up, if you get 3 incorrect answers in a row, you will move a level down.

There is a Show Answer button that is available to view the correct answer only three times during one game.
